We’re excited to have you attend our workshop! After you signed up here, please read the following:

What you should bring

  • a laptop
  • your laptop’s charger
  • money for drinks and lunch
  • your enthusiasm and open mind

Tools to download

Please install the following apps on your laptop before the workshop. If you run into trouble, we’ll help you with the installation at the workshop.

Browsers

  • Chrome
  • Firefox

You can see if you have the latest version of your browser by visiting the whatbrowser page.

Texteditor

Sublime Text 2

How to get to the venue

The workshop is located on co.up’s 3rd floor.
Co.up is a co-working space located at Adalbertstraße 8, 10999 Berlin, near the U-bahn station Kottbusser Tor.
We’re in the backyard, so study the map here to find out how to find us: http://i.imgur.com/k0Of1sg.png.
At the doors please ring the bell for co.up’s 3rd floor. After we buzz you in, please use the stairs to come find us.

Food and drinks

At 1pm we will an hour-long lunch break. You can bring something to eat, order something to be delivered, or go out to eat. There are lots of vegan and vegetarian options around us.

Co.up has soft drinks, beer, Club Mate, and bottled water for sale. We can show our support by buying drinks from them, but you’re welcome to bring your own .
